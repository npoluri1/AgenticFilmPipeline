SCENE 1 - INT. TEMPLE COURTYARD - DAY

BACKGROUND: Ancient temple courtyard in Tirupati, morning sunlight

RAMAIAH: Namaskaram! The festival preparations must be completed by sunset.
LAKSHMI: Yes Anna, the villagers have gathered all the flowers and decorations.
RAMAIAH: Good. Ensure every diya is lit before the priest arrives.
NARRATOR: The ancient traditions of Telugu land live on through generations.

SCENE 2 - INT. VILLAGE SQUARE - EVENING

BACKGROUND: Bustling village square with colorful stalls and crowds

VENKAT: The dance troupe has arrived from Kuchipudi.
RAMAIAH: Excellent. Let them prepare for the evening performance.
LAKSHMI: I will arrange the seating for the elders.
VENKAT: The musicians are tuning their instruments.

SCENE 3 - EXT. RIVERBANK - SUNSET

BACKGROUND: Peaceful Godavari riverbank with golden sunset

NARRATOR: As the sun sets over the ancient river, centuries of tradition flow on.
RAMAIAH: This land holds the stories of our ancestors.
LAKSHMI: And we must pass them to the next generation.
VENKAT: The children are ready to learn the old songs.
